Lorrogami

Here is how to make your very own Mask of Lorro origami:
  1. Print this picture and cut it out.
  2. Fold along line A to make a triangle. Make sure Lorro's face is on the outside.
  3. Flip it over so Lorro's face looks at the table. Fold on the B lines so the edges match up in the middle. Now it looks like a kite.
  4. Now it is time to make the ears. Fold on line C so the flap tucks inside.
  5. Fold towards the center on line D. You can see her ear sticking up.
  6. Now, you fold line E up. Turn it over and you're done!
