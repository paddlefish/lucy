What you need:

  • Print one copy of the playing pieces
  • Scissors

1. Carefully cut out the "Find the Biscuit" cards and the biscuit playing piece.

2. Place the biscuits piece on the table and cover it up with one of the cards.

3. Put the other cards on the table too.

4. Scramble up the cards by sliding them around the table. Make sure the biscuit piece doesn't show! Try to keep it hiding under a card.

5. Have a friend guess where the biscuits are and then take turns hiding them again.

LUCY TRIVIA: Lucy actually plays a game like this with real milkbones! If you hide one in your hand and then hold out your fists, she will try to pick the hand that has the treat. She's pretty good at it, but sometimes her nose fools her!!
