 Lucy Paper Doll

What you need:

scissors

crayons or colored pencils

glue

plain paper

light weight cardboard

1. Print out the Lucy doll and color it.

2. Glue your colored picture to some light weight cardboard and carefully cut it out.

3. Print out Lucy's outfits and color them.

4. Carefully cut them out.

5. Have fun with Lucy by folding the tabs over and putting on her stylish new outfits!
