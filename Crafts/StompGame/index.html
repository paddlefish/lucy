Lucy's Sunny Summer Stomp Game!

Click here and then print the game board and playing pieces.

Cut out the game board and playing pieces. You might like to glue the pieces to some lightweight cardboard before you play.

How to Play:
1. Each player puts markers on half of the points of the star. Milkbones go on one side of the blue line and footprints go on the other.


2. The youngest player goes first and can move one piece from a red sun ray to the empty center space. On your first turn, you have to start from a red sun ray, but after that you can move any piece.


3. Take turns moving your pieces. You can move from the center to any empty sun ray or you can move from a sun ray to the center. You can also move from a sun ray to any open sun ray next to it (no skipping over sun rays).

4. Your goal is to try and block the other player, so they have nowhere to move. The game ends when one player is stuck and can't move a piece.
