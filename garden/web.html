The Violet Showcase
  A greenhouse in Englewood, CO where I got the starters for my collection. Very nice staff and beautiful plants! I had such a hard time choosing! I must give credit for the borrowed violet graphic (used in the title) from their site.

www.violetshowcase.com

 Rachel's Reflections
  A site with great how-to's and lots of pictures on everything related to growing violets.

www.RachelsReflections.com

 Rob's Mini-o-lets
  Rob's specializes in miniature violets. I have one Rob's mini in bud (!) which is a beautiful plant. They also have a great culture corner with how-to's and more.

www.RobsViolet.com

 Lyndon Lyon Greenhouse
  Another great website from one of the pioneers in violet growing.

 www.lyndonlyon.com

 Florals of Fredericks
  Their online catalog is enormous with good descriptions of the varieties they carry.

 www.african-violets.com

 Optimara Violets
 The best feature of this site is "Dr. Optimara", a resource for diagnosing various violet ailments.

 www.optimara.com
