Play Jeopar-Dog! and test your trivia knowledge!


Instructions: Enter your name, then wait for the game to load. When the scoreboard is shown, click on the dollar value in the category you want to answer. When the question is shown, buzz in by pressing return or by clicking the "buzz in" button. Quickly type your answer, the Jeopardy-style "what is..." is not required. Occasionally, Lucy will buzz in too! Your name will turn red if you were the first to buzz in.
