Once upon a time, Lucy went for a walk. First she plastered some fences and barked at a biscuit. Suddenly, Bubba the fox from upstairs jumped out and knuckled! Soon, however, they became friends. Together they started towards Vermillion when they spied 87 scorned dumbells looming on the sidewalk. 'Phooey!' shouted Lucy. Bubba ran to the left, and Lucy ran to the right, and soon they had the dumbells cornered. They were about to tear the dumbells limb from limb when Andy and Becka said 'Lucy, get over here'. And when Lucy came in she got a big treat, a '57 Vincet Black Lightning! Boy, did Lucy sleep well that night!
